About Me

I am currently a 3rd-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student, majoring in Mobile and Web Application Development. My passion for technology and creativity has driven me to explore various aspects of software development, multimedia, and design. This portfolio serves as a curated showcase of my work from the course Multimedia and Hypermedia Systems, reflecting my journey in mastering both the technical and creative sides of IT.

Whether it’s building interactive web applications or creating stunning digital art, my work reflects a blend of creativity and problem-solving.
